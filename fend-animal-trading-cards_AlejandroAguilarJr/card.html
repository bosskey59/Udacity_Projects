Prisma Bear !!!!

Although extremely bright and colorful, this bear has only been seen... and photographed once.

  • Scientific Name: Colursus arctos
  • Average Length: 10 ft.
  • Average Lifespan: Eternal
  • Habitat: dense forests, subalpine meadows, open plains and arctic tundra.

With all the colors of the rainbow on its coat, this is the most unique bear in existance. Primarily a solitary creature, it lives in the most secluded areas of this earth. Although not many facts are known about this creature, some believe it to be friendly in nature.
